# Threads-Unfollowers

[![maintained](https://img.shields.io/badge/maintained-yes-green.svg)](https://github.com/timuc7n/Threads-Unfollowers)
[![license](https://img.shields.io/badge/license-MIT-blue.svg)](LICENSE)
[![open-source](https://img.shields.io/badge/community-friendly-orange.svg)](https://github.com/timuc7n/Threads-Unfollowers)

A clean, fast, and open-source script to identify and unfollow users who do not follow you back on Threads. Browser-based, requires no downloads, and is completely free to use, copy, and modify.

## ⚠️ WARNING
This project is for educational purposes only. Use it at your own risk. Excessive use might lead to temporary limitations or bans on your account. We are not affiliated with Meta or Threads.

## 🚀 Why Threads-Unfollowers?
- **Human-Like Interaction:** Designed with built-in delays to mimic human behavior, minimizing the risk of account flags.
- **High Performance:** Optimized algorithms to scan thousands of followers in seconds without memory leaks.
- **Privacy First:** The script runs strictly locally in your browser. We have zero access to your data, cookies, or account credentials.
- **Zero Installation:** Forget sketchy browser extensions; simply copy, paste, and run in your console.
- **Universal Compatibility:** Works flawlessly on Windows, macOS, and Linux across all modern browsers (Chrome, Firefox, Edge, Safari).
- **Mobile Ready:** Perfectly functional on desktop browsers; follow the simple steps below to clean your list on the go.

## 🤝 Open Source & Community-Friendly
We believe in transparency. This project is 100% open-source. Feel free to inspect, copy, modify, or contribute to the code.

## 🖥️ How to Use / Nasıl Kullanılır?

### [EN] English Version
1. **Visit:** Go to our [Web Interface](https://timuc7n.github.io/Threads-Unfollowers/).
2. **Select & Copy:** Select your language (TR or EN) and click the big **COPY** button to copy the script.
3. **Open Threads:** Go to [Threads.com](https://threads.com) and log in to your account.
4. **Console:** Press `F12` on your keyboard (or right-click anywhere, select "Inspect", then click the "Console" tab).
5. **Run:** Paste the code into the console and press `Enter`. The script will start working automatically!

### [TR] Türkçe Versiyon
1. **Siteye Git:** [Web Arayüzümüze](https://timuc7n.github.io/Threads-Unfollowers/) gidin.
2. **Seç ve Kopyala:** Dilinizi (TR veya EN) seçin ve büyük **COPY** butonuna tıklayarak kodu kopyalayın.
3. **Threads'i Aç:** [Threads.com](https://threads.com) adresine gidin ve hesabınıza giriş yapın.
4. **Konsolu Aç:** Klavyenizden `F12` tuşuna basın (veya herhangi bir yere sağ tıklayıp "İncele" deyin, ardından açılan pencerede "Console" sekmesine tıklayın).
5. **Çalıştır:** Kopyaladığınız kodu konsol kısmına yapıştırın ve `Enter` tuşuna basın. Script otomatik olarak çalışmaya başlayacaktır!
